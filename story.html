Story

MHKlara is a dream by four Engineering Students earlier in 2014. "The true art of storytelling involves writing active characters who engage the audience" this was the inspiration to continue our work on the story by then. The name 'MHKlara' was an accident to us, we never came up with a better name than this.

"Each success has some failure along the way", we believed in this. At first when we planned to shoot some of the videos we never able to execute the plan. This picture was in the year 2014 where we have the shooting for the first time and this was our poster.

Later, after three years in 2017 again we are back with ideas and started to shoot. We completed the shoot, but again we failed could not publish online. These failures taught us some many things and we fixed our mindset until we come up with the complete plan we will never shoot again.

After 7 long years, we finally managed to put a video online. The vision of MHKlara is to entertain people and to build quality of filming around. After 7 long years, we finally managed to put a video online. The vision of MHKlara is to entertain people and to build quality of filming around.
